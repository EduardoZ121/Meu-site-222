REMAKE PIXEL — TOOLS & SECTIONS OVERVIEW
For: Marketing / content creator briefing
Excludes: Video tool and Manga/Scene Flow editor
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

This document explains every section and tool inside remakepix.com —
what it does, the goal, and what users gain from it.
All tools work in-browser with no installation, no Photoshop, no
design skills required. Pay-per-use credits, no subscription.


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. EASY MODE  (also called "Estúdio" or "Studio")
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

What it does:
Type a simple idea in plain language → AI generates a polished,
professional-quality image in seconds.

The goal:
Replace expensive stock photos, freelance designers and hours of
Photoshop with a single sentence. The user types what they imagine,
the AI delivers it.

What people gain:
- Original visuals for posts, blogs, ads, products — no copyright issues
- Save €30–€100+ per image vs hiring a designer/illustrator
- Get a usable result in under 15 seconds
- 96 curated style presets so the output never looks "generic AI"
- Multiple aspect ratios (square, vertical for Reels/Stories, landscape)

Best use cases:
- Instagram feed posts, Reels covers, Stories backgrounds
- Blog header images
- Ad creatives (Meta/Google Ads)
- Mood boards, presentation visuals
- Personal creative projects


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2. PRO MODE  (Photorealistic Refinement)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

What it does:
Upload your own photo + choose a preset → AI refines it into a
photorealistic, magazine-quality version of itself.

The goal:
Turn an average phone photo into something that looks like it came
from a professional photoshoot — same person, same scene, just
elevated.

What people gain:
- Make personal photos look like they were taken by a pro
- Clean up imperfections without losing identity
- Match lighting/mood to a specific aesthetic
- Useful for LinkedIn headshots, dating profiles, model portfolios,
  brand founder shots

Best use cases:
- Profile pictures (LinkedIn, Instagram, dating apps)
- "About me" / founder portraits for websites
- E-commerce product shots from amateur photos
- Wedding/event photos that need a polish


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3. ARTISTIC MODE  (A photo, reimagined)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

What it does:
Upload a photo + pick one of 33 artistic styles → AI transforms the
photo into that style while keeping the subject recognizable.

Styles include: oil painting, watercolor, anime, comic book, Renaissance
portrait, cyberpunk, vintage 35mm film, fashion editorial, B&W
photojournalism, neon noir, and many more.

The goal:
Turn ordinary photos into shareable, eye-catching art pieces that
stop the scroll.

What people gain:
- Instantly transform photos into wall-art quality images
- Create themed content series (e.g. "my friends as Renaissance paintings")
- Strong viral potential — transformation content gets high engagement
- Make personal/family photos special for gifts and prints

Best use cases:
- Viral Instagram/TikTok "before-after" reels
- Gift ideas (printed canvas of someone in oil-painting style)
- Personal aesthetic content
- Author/musician promotional photos


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
4. POSTERS  (44 marketing templates)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

What it does:
Choose a poster template (event, sale, restaurant, fitness, tech,
corporate, etc.) → fill in your details (date, name, headline) →
AI generates a designed poster matching the chosen aesthetic.

The goal:
Give small businesses, event organizers and creators access to
designer-quality posters without hiring an agency.

What people gain:
- Professional-looking posters in 30 seconds
- 44 templates covering every industry (food, gym, club night, sale,
  workshop, real estate, etc.)
- Save the typical €50–€200 charged by freelance designers per poster
- Consistent visual quality across all marketing materials

Best use cases:
- Restaurant daily specials / weekend events
- Fitness studios announcing classes or promotions
- Club / event promoters (party flyers)
- Small e-commerce sales announcements
- Real estate listings
- Workshop / course promotion


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
5. CAROUSEL GENERATOR
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

What it does:
Type a topic or paste a script → AI generates a complete multi-slide
Instagram/LinkedIn carousel (cover + content slides + CTA), each
visually coordinated.

The goal:
Solve one of the most painful tasks for content creators: making
visually consistent multi-slide carousels that don't look amateur.

What people gain:
- Full carousel in under 1 minute (vs. 1–2 hours in Canva)
- Visually coordinated slides (same style, same vibe)
- High-engagement format proven to boost reach on Instagram/LinkedIn
- Perfect for educational, list-style, or storytelling content

Best use cases:
- "5 tips for…" educational posts
- Product feature breakdowns
- Behind-the-scenes storytelling
- Quote series / motivational posts
- Step-by-step tutorials
- Company updates / announcements


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
6. UTILITY TOOLS  (6 single-purpose AI tools)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

These are quick, one-click tools for specific image needs:

  6.1 BACKGROUND REMOVAL
      Removes the background from any photo, leaves only the subject.
      → Use for: product photos for e-commerce, profile pics with
        transparent background, sticker creation, design overlays.
      → Saves: €5–€15 per image vs paid services like remove.bg

  6.2 PHOTO UPSCALE
      Increases image resolution (2x or 4x) without losing quality.
      → Use for: making old/small photos printable, enhancing
        screenshots, preparing images for billboards or large prints.
      → Saves: hours in Photoshop or the cost of professional retouching.

  6.3 PHOTO RESTORE
      Repairs old, damaged, scratched or blurry photos. Brings back
      lost detail, fixes faces, removes scratches.
      → Use for: restoring family heirloom photos, fixing damaged
        scans, bringing old wedding/childhood photos back to life.
      → Emotional value + practical value combined.

  6.4 COLORIZE
      Turns black-and-white photos into realistic color images.
      → Use for: bringing historical photos to life, family archives,
        creative B&W → color reveals on social media.
      → Strong viral potential when paired with old family photos.

  6.5 INPAINT  (Magic Eraser)
      Paint over anything you want removed from a photo — AI fills
      the gap naturally. Remove unwanted people, objects, text,
      watermarks, blemishes.
      → Use for: cleaning up vacation photos, removing photobombers,
        deleting old logos, cleaning up product shots.

  6.6 CLOTHES CHANGER
      Upload a photo of a person + describe new clothing → AI
      swaps the outfit while keeping the person identical.
      → Use for: fashion e-commerce mockups, "try this look" content,
        outfit visualization before buying, avatar customization,
        editorial fashion creation.


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
7. WIZARD  (Guided creation for beginners)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

What it does:
A step-by-step questionnaire that asks the user simple questions
("What style? What mood? What colors? Who's in it?") and builds a
detailed AI prompt automatically. Best for users who don't know
how to describe what they want.

The goal:
Remove the "blank page" problem — give beginners a guided path to
great results without needing to learn prompt engineering.

What people gain:
- No prompt writing skills required
- Consistently better results vs. typing random words
- Discover styles and moods they didn't know existed
- Feel confident from day one


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
8. GALLERY  (Personal library)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

What it does:
Every generated image is automatically saved in the user's personal
gallery. Users can browse history, mark favorites, redownload,
re-edit, or share their best work.

The goal:
Make sure users never lose a great image and can return to past
creations anytime.

What people gain:
- Cloud-saved history of all creations
- Filter by tool, style, date or favorite
- Re-use successful prompts as starting points
- Build a personal creative portfolio over time


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
9. FAVORITES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

What it does:
A curated subset of the gallery — users mark their best images as
favorites for quick access later.

The goal:
Quick re-access to top creations without scrolling through hundreds
of past generations.

What people gain:
- Find best work instantly
- Build a "best-of" portfolio for personal/professional use


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
10. CREDITS / BILLING
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

What it does:
The pricing model. Users buy credits (one-time purchases via Stripe)
and spend them per generation. No subscription, no auto-renewal.

Credit costs (approximate):
- Simple image (Easy Mode): 1–5 credits
- Pro / Artistic / Photorealistic image: 10–15 credits
- Poster: 5–10 credits
- Carousel: 15–25 credits (depending on slide count)
- Background removal / upscale / restore / colorize: 1–3 credits

Free credits:
- New users: 50 free credits at signup
- Referral bonus: +10 credits per friend invited
- Credits never expire

The goal:
Make the platform accessible without subscription anxiety. Pay
only for what you create — no monthly fee, no commitment.

What people gain:
- Try without risk (50 free credits)
- No surprise charges
- Use as little or as much as needed
- Bring friends to get more free credits (organic growth lever)


───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
11. REFERRALS  (Invite friends)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

What it does:
Each user gets a personal referral code/link. When a friend signs
up using their link and creates their first image, both receive
+10 credits.

The goal:
Built-in viral growth mechanism. Reward existing users for spreading
the word, lower customer acquisition cost.

What people gain:
- Earn free credits by inviting friends
- No limit on how many friends they can refer
- Real-time stats on who joined through their link
